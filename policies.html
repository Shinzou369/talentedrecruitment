Privacy Policy

Talented Recruitment ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Talented Recruitment is a recruitment business registered in England and Wales (Company No. 16642544), based in Rugby, United Kingdom. We act as the Data Controller for all personal data collected in the course of our recruitment operations.

2. Data We Collect

We collect personal data that you provide to us, including:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number)
  • CV and employment history
  • Education, skills, and qualifications
  • References and correspondence relating to job opportunities

We do not process sensitive data (such as health, ethnicity, or diversity data).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Match candidates with suitable job opportunities
  • Communicate with clients and candidates
  • Maintain business relationships
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent - for candidates who register or apply for roles
  • Legitimate interests - for clients and business contacts
  • Legal obligation - for record-keeping and compliance

5. Data Retention

We retain candidate data until consent is withdrawn. Clients' business contact data is retained for as long as is necessary to maintain our business relationship.

6. Data Security

We store personal data securely using encrypted systems and restrict access to authorised personnel only. Our third-party CRM platform is hosted within the UK/EU and complies with UK GDPR standards.

7. Data Sharing

We only share personal data with potential employers, service providers, and third-party platforms necessary to deliver our recruitment services. All third parties are bound by data protection agreements to ensure security and confidentiality.

8. International Transfers

We do not transfer personal data outside the UK or EEA. Where this becomes necessary, we will ensure equivalent safeguards are in place, such as those under the UK-US Data Bridge.

9. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

You can contact us at amelia@talentedrecruitment.co.uk to exercise these rights.

10. Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our handling of your data, you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at www.ico.org.uk.

Modern Slavery and Human Trafficking Statement

Issued under Section 54 of the Modern Slavery Act 2015

Introduction

This statement sets out the steps that Talented Recruitment has taken, and will continue to take, to ensure that modern slavery and human trafficking are not taking place within our business or supply chains.

Talented Recruitment has a zero-tolerance approach to any form of modern slavery. We are committed to acting ethically, transparently, and with integrity in all our business dealings, and to implementing effective systems and controls to prevent exploitation of people in any part of our operations.

Our Business and Structure

Talented Recruitment is a UK-based recruitment and talent consultancy supporting clients across multiple sectors.

Our Commitment

We will not tolerate, permit, or condone the use of forced, bonded, or involuntary labour in any form. We also prohibit child labour, servitude, and human trafficking in our operations and expect the same standards from every organisation we work with.

We are committed to:

  • Conducting business only with suppliers and partners who uphold high ethical and labour standards
  • Ensuring all recruitment practices are fair, transparent, and voluntary
  • Maintaining awareness of modern slavery risks within our industry
  • Taking prompt action if we identify or suspect any breach of these principles

Our Policies and Controls

To reinforce our commitment, we have established a set of internal policies and procedures, including:

  • Modern Slavery and Human Trafficking Policy (sets clear reporting expectations and supplier standards)
  • Ethical Policy and Corporate Social Responsibility Policy (ensure integrity in decision-making)
  • Recruitment and Employment Policies (require fair, voluntary, and lawful employment practices)
  • Whistleblowing and Grievance Policy (provides safe routes for raising concerns without fear of reprisal)

Risk Assessment and Due Diligence

While our operations are low-risk by nature, we recognise that recruitment and supply chain activities can present vulnerabilities to modern slavery. To mitigate these:

  • We review supplier relationships to ensure fair labour standards
  • We conduct background checks on new partners and service providers
  • We include ethical-compliance clauses in supplier terms where applicable
  • We monitor for irregularities in pay, working hours, or conditions that may signal exploitation

Training and Awareness

All staff receive information about identifying and reporting potential modern slavery risks. Managers involved in recruitment or supplier engagement receive specific guidance on due diligence and reporting responsibilities. We continue to promote awareness through internal communications and updates during onboarding and policy reviews.

Future Actions and Continuous Improvement

We will continue to strengthen our approach through:

  • Regular policy and supplier reviews
  • Ongoing staff training and awareness
  • Improved supplier vetting and documentation
  • Enhanced reporting processes and data monitoring

We understand that tackling modern slavery requires ongoing vigilance and continuous improvement - not just compliance.

Approval

This statement has been approved by the Managing Director of Talented Recruitment and will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary.

Anti-Bribery and Corruption Policy

Introduction

Talented Recruitment operates a zero-tolerance policy towards bribery and corruption. We conduct all business fairly, honestly, and transparently in line with the Bribery Act 2010.

Purpose

To ensure employees, contractors, and partners act with integrity and do not engage in or tolerate bribery or improper influence.

What You Need to Know

  • No employee may offer, give, request, or accept any bribe or facilitation payment
  • Hospitality and gifts are only acceptable if they are modest, appropriate, and recorded
  • All concerns about potential bribery must be reported to a Managing Director
  • Talented Recruitment will end relationships with any party involved in corrupt activity

Responsibility

Management ensures compliance and investigates any allegations of misconduct.

Review

Reviewed annually or when legislative changes occur.

Ethical Policy

Introduction

At Talented Recruitment, we believe that success is built on integrity, fairness, and respect. We expect every decision and interaction to reflect our commitment to ethical business practice.

Purpose

To provide clear expectations of professional and ethical conduct within Talented Recruitment and across our supply chain.

What You Need to Know

  • We act lawfully, fairly, and transparently in all dealings
  • We expect honesty, respect, and fairness in every client and candidate relationship
  • We work only with suppliers and partners who meet similar ethical standards
  • Any ethical concern can be raised through the Grievance and Whistleblowing Policy

Corporate Social Responsibility

Talented Recruitment is committed to being a responsible business. We value our people, support our community, and strive to make a positive social and environmental impact.

Anti-Money Laundering

We comply fully with UK anti-money-laundering regulations. Talented Recruitment conducts business transparently, verifies client and candidate identity, and does not engage in or facilitate any unlawful financial activity.

Data Protection and Social Media Policy

Introduction

This policy applies to any employee of Talented Recruitment or any company owned by the Managing Director that chooses to adopt these policies.

Talented Recruitment handles data, technology, and communication with care and integrity. We're a people business, and the trust of our clients, candidates, and colleagues depends on how responsibly we manage information and use technology.

We expect everyone to protect personal and company data, use technology sensibly, and communicate in a way that reflects our professionalism and values.

Data Protection

Talented Recruitment is committed to processing all personal data in line with the principles of the UK GDPR, which require that data be:

  • Processed lawfully, fairly and transparently
  • Collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes
  • Adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary
  • Accurate and kept up to date
  • Retained only as long as necessary
  • Kept secure and protected from unauthorised access or loss

We collect and process data about employees, candidates, and clients for legitimate business purposes, including recruitment, payroll, performance management, and compliance.
